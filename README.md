# voronoi-traditions

**Voronoi tessellation of cultural traditions** — partition the space of cultural traditions using Voronoi diagrams computed from spectral distance matrices.

## What This Gives You

- **Voronoi tessellation** — partition tradition space into natural neighborhoods
- **Weighted diagrams** — account for tradition prominence and influence
- **Visualization** — 2D and 3D renderings of the tradition landscape
- **Boundary analysis** — identify traditions at the intersection of cultural regions

## How It Fits

Geometric companion to `betti-music-computation` and `dial-space-explorer`. The Voronoi cells provide a complementary view to the topological structures from persistent homology.
